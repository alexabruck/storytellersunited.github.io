Telling stories born on the web

We are an international community of media makers, technologists and designers interested in innovative forms of storytelling.

Storytelling has been with us since humans gathered around the campfire. As our media evolved through the ages, the way we tell stories has developed. The web enables stories to be interactive, dynamic and participatory, empowering experiences previously left untold.

We are 500+ strong on Slack, hailing from different timezones, skillsets and professional backgrounds. We love sharing; from upcoming events to projects we're working on and from useful tools to exciting opportunities.

Join us on Slack

We partner with organisations to explore the future of storytelling in quick, hands-on, multi-disciplinary sprints. We call these events 'Hack Jams': they mix story, code and creativity with fun and community. These events bring together teams, combining different skills to create innovative story prototypes that are born on the web.
